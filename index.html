Our buildings

Academic building 1


The Academic Building 1 serves as a central and pivotal space for various academic functions and activities.
This modern and architecturally striking building is designed to cater to the diverse needs of students and faculty, promoting collaborative learning, innovation, and engagement. Here's a breakdown of what the Academic Building offers:

1. Lecture Halls and Classrooms, 2. Faculty and Administrative Offices
3. Study and Collaboration Spaces, 4. Research Facilities and Labs
5. Multimedia and Technology Centers, 6. Academic Support Services
7. Event Spaces.

Academic building 2


Building 2 at RMIT Vietnam's Saigon campus is a hub of academic resources, research materials, and collaborative learning spaces.
This modern and welcoming facility is designed to support students and faculty in their pursuit of knowledge, research, and intellectual growth.
These are some rooms that are inside Building 2:

1. Extensive Collection of Resources, 2. Study Areas and Reading Rooms,
3. Digital Resources and Technology, 4. Research Support Services,
5. Collaboration and Academic Workshops, 6. Special Collections and Archives.

Building 8

Building 8 at RMIT Vietnam's Saigon campus combines academic and residential functions. With classrooms, a Circle K shop, and student accommodations, the building provides a seamless blend of convenience and educational resources, promoting an efficient and vibrant campus experience. These are what inside building 8:

1. Commercial Space: Circle K Shop
2. Study Spaces/Classrooms
3. Student Accommodation

Building 9

Building 9 appears to be a multifunctional facility that not only provides essential living spaces for students but also offers additional amenities to enhance their overall experience. The combination of a restaurant, laundry and an entertainment/relaxation zone with various activities.
The reception area further emphasizes the building's role in managing accommodation services and maintaining a supportive living environment for students on campus.
inside building 9, these are the offer:

1. Restaurant
2. Student Accommodation
3. Laundry
4. Entertainment/Relax Zone with Billiards, Air Hockey, Table Football, Automat with Food, Computers and Sofas
5. Reception (Management) of the Accommodation.

Sports Hall


The Sports and Recreational Facilities Building at RMIT Vietnam's Saigon campus is a vibrant and dynamic space dedicated to promoting physical wellness, sportsmanship, and recreational activities.
This modern facility is designed to accommodate a wide range of athletic and leisure pursuits, providing students with opportunities to engage in active and healthy lifestyles.
These are some usage for the REC or known as the sports hall:

1. Athletic Grounds and Courts, 2. Fitness Centers and Gymnasiums,
3. Multi-Purpose Indoor Sports Arena 4. Wellness and Recreation Programs,
5. Supportive Facilities
