SPORTS HALL


The Sports and Recreational Facilities Building at RMIT Vietnam's Saigon campus is a vibrant and dynamic space dedicated to promoting physical wellness, sportsmanship, and recreational activities.
This modern facility is designed to accommodate a wide range of athletic and leisure pursuits, providing students with opportunities to engage in active and healthy lifestyles.
These are some usage for the REC or known as the sports hall:
1. Athletic Grounds and Courts
2. Fitness Centers and Gymnasiums
3. Multi-Purpose Indoor Sports Arena
4. Wellness and Recreation Programs
5. Supportive Facilities
Overall, the Sports and Recreational Facilities Building at RMIT Vietnam's Saigon campus is a vibrant and integral part of the campus life, promoting a healthy and balanced lifestyle for students through sports, fitness, and recreational activities.
